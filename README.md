# AI Job Market Trend Analysis (Excel Dashboard)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes trends in the Artificial Intelligence (AI) job market using an interactive Excel dashboard. The dataset contains 2000 AI-related job postings with information about Company information , Industry domain, Job titles & experience levels, Required skills & tools, Salary ranges (USD), Location & employment type and Posting dates (2023–2025).

The goal of this project is to identify key insights about the AI job market, including job demand, salary trends, and industry hiring patterns.

## Dashboard Features
The Excel dashboard includes interactive filters and visualizations to explore the AI job market.

Key dashboard components:

- **AI Job Market Trend Over Time**  
  Shows how AI job postings change across years.

- **Jobs by Experience Level**  
  Distribution of jobs across Entry, Mid, and Senior levels.

- **Jobs by Industry**  
  Industries with the highest demand for AI professionals.

- **Average Salary by Job Title**  
  Comparison of salary levels across different AI roles.

- **Top 10 Companies with High Salaries**  
  Companies offering higher salary packages.

- **Jobs by Employment Type**  
  Full-time, contract, remote, and internship roles.

Interactive **slicers** allow filtering by:
- Industry
- Job Title
- Experience Level

---

## Key Insights
Some insights from the analysis include:

- Data Analyst and  NLP engineering roles are among the most demanded positions and has the highest salary.
- Technology and Retail industries show strong hiring demand for AI professionals.
- AI job opportunities have shown increasing demand over recent years and when forcasted it for 2026.
